MEET THE FOUNDER

Hi, I'm Kresha Hirani.

The Bombay Lip Co. was born from a simple belief—beauty should never feel one-size-fits-all.

After years of searching for lipstick shades that truly complemented different skin tones, I decided to create something different. Every product is thoughtfully handcrafted in small batches, combining luxurious ingredients with personalisation to make every customer feel unique.

From nourishing lip balms and exfoliating lip scrubs to completely customised lipsticks, every creation is made with care, passion and attention to detail.

Thank you for supporting a dream built with love. I hope every product brings a little everyday luxury into your life.
