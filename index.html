MEET THE FOUNDER

Crafted with Passion. Inspired by Individuality.

Hi, I'm Kresha Hirani, founder of The Bombay Lip Co. What began as a simple curiosity about handmade lip care soon became a passion to create beauty products as unique as the people who wear them.

Like many women, I struggled to find lipstick shades that truly complemented my skin tone. That simple frustration inspired me to create a brand where every lipstick is customised to the individual — from the perfect shade and finish to fragrance and packaging.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches with carefully selected ingredients. Alongside bespoke lipsticks, we create nourishing lip balms, exfoliating lip scrubs and luxurious lip care that celebrates self-expression and confidence.

Building The Bombay Lip Co. while working a corporate career wasn't easy. It demanded endless learning, research and experimentation, but with the unwavering support of my husband and family, a dream slowly transformed into the brand you see today.

Thank you for supporting handmade craftsmanship. I hope every product reminds you that luxury feels even more special when it's made just for you.
