MEET THE FOUNDER

Hi, I'm Kresha Hirani.

The Bombay Lip Co. began with one simple belief — luxury beauty should always feel personal.

After years of searching for lipstick shades that truly complemented different skin tones, I realised there had to be a better way. That journey inspired me to create handcrafted lip care and customised lipsticks made exclusively for each individual.

Every lip balm, lip scrub and lipstick is lovingly handmade in small batches using thoughtfully selected ingredients, ensuring every customer receives something created with care.

Building this brand alongside my professional career has been a journey filled with passion, patience and creativity. Every order reminds me why this dream began.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know

Are all your products handmade?

Yes. Every lip balm, lip scrub and customised lipstick is handcrafted in small batches to ensure exceptional quality and freshness.

Can I customise my lipstick?

Absolutely. We customise the shade, undertone and finish based on your preferences.

How long does a customised lipstick take?

As every lipstick is handmade exclusively for you, production generally takes a few working days before dispatch.

Do you accept gifting or bulk orders?

Yes. We create customised lip care for bridal hampers, corporate gifting, return favours and special occasions.
